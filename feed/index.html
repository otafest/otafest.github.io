Brendan Hunter

Voice Actor Guest

Welcome back to Brendan Hunter! Alberta’s son; born here he has been able to have a glorious career living and staying here, too. Brendan has been a pro VA since 2003, starting as UUB in Dragon Ball GT. Perhaps best known as the original HISOKA from the Ocean Dub’s of Hunter X Hunter, and as TIEN SHINHAN of Dragon Ball (on YTV across Canada till 2006). Otafest gave him his first professional Convention Guesting opportunity as a Voice Actor in 2013 and Brendan has been touring North American conventions ever since. Surprisingly, voicing the GARDENSCAPES phone game lead; AUSTIN the Butler, has been dubbed “the worst villain in gaming”. Villains have been a specialty in Brendan’s acting career. But don’t let his curly moustache fool you; he’s a great friend to congoers, the kind Lead Voice Acting teacher on closingcredtis.com, and feels like the Otafest team are family after these numerous invitations to their world class event.
